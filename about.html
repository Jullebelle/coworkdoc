Our mission

Coworkdoc means co-working with doctors. We are a Chilean research and development company focused on surgical medical devices. We listen to surgeons, interpret their requirements, and engineer solutions that don't exist anywhere else on the market.

Every instrument we develop starts with a conversation — a surgeon describing an unmet clinical need, a procedure limited by inadequate tooling, an outcome that could be better. We translate those conversations into purpose-built instruments.

  • Better quality of life for patients through purpose-built surgical instruments
  • Working together with surgeons to improve medical techniques
  • Aligned with the needs of diverse health systems across Latin America
