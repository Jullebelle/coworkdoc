We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including Chile's Ley 19.628 on the Protection of Private Life and, where applicable, the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

3. How we use your information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your product enquiry or request for information
  • Connect you with the appropriate regional distributor if applicable
  • Follow up on your enquiry if you have requested a callback

We do not use your personal information for marketing communications, profiling, or automated decision-making. We will never sell your information to third parties.

4. Legal basis for processing

Where GDPR applies (for example, if you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area), we process your personal information on the following legal basis:

  • Legitimate interests — responding to an enquiry you have submitted constitutes a legitimate interest in maintaining business communications.
  • Consent — by submitting the contact form, you consent to us using your information to respond to your request.

Under Chilean law (Ley 19.628), we process your data with your consent, provided through voluntary submission of the contact form.

5. Third parties

We may share your information with:

  • Netlify — our website hosting provider, which processes contact form submissions on our behalf. Netlify is based in the United States and processes data in accordance with its own privacy policy.
  • Matching Deals — our Latin America commercial distribution partner, if your enquiry relates to product availability or distribution in your region.

We do not share your information with any other third parties. We do not use advertising networks, marketing platforms, or social media tracking pixels on this website.

6. How long we keep your information

We retain contact form submissions for as long as is necessary to respond to your enquiry and maintain a record of our communications — typically no longer than 2 years from the date of submission unless an ongoing commercial relationship requires otherwise.

You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us at ventas@coworkdoc.cl.

7. Cookies

This website uses a single functional cookie to remember your language preference (English or Spanish). This cookie contains no personal information, is not shared with any third party, and expires at the end of your browser session.

We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party tracking technologies. No consent banner is required for strictly functional cookies of this type.

You can disable cookies entirely in your browser settings, although this may affect the language preference feature.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal information
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Portability — receive your information in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at ventas@coworkdoc.cl. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are located in Chile and believe we have not handled your personal information in accordance with Ley 19.628, you may lodge a complaint with the relevant Chilean regulatory authority.

9. Security

This website is served over HTTPS, ensuring that data transmitted between your browser and our server is encrypted. Contact form submissions are handled by Netlify Forms, which employs industry-standard security measures.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
